# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and empathetic.
- Give and accept technical feedback in good faith.
- Focus criticism on ideas and observable behavior.
- Respect privacy, boundaries, and requests to stop.
- Take responsibility, apologize, and repair harm when mistakes occur.

Harassment, threats, sexualized attention, discriminatory language, doxxing, sustained disruption, and retaliation are unacceptable in project spaces or when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate this code. Reports should be sent privately through the security contact method in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) when no separate conduct contact is published. Maintainers will protect reporter privacy as far as reasonably possible and may apply a warning, temporary restriction, permanent ban, or other proportionate consequence.

This code is based on the principles of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 and is released under CC BY 4.0.
