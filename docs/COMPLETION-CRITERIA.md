This ledger separates the current open-source public-beta candidate from historical package/CI evidence and still-pending publication or manual hardware work. The candidate adds explicit launch-at-login consent, hardened local profile/import file access, fail-closed schema/snapshot boundaries, a complete-history/asset public-release audit, a locally verified bilingual public surface, recoverable exact-candidate draft preparation, two-phase remote-controls evidence, and approval-bound publication tooling. It passed 3,046 non-live deterministic checks/assertions—506 app checks plus 2,540 release-tooling assertions—and produced an unsigned development-evidence package; nothing was deployed, pushed, tagged, or published. The initial beta is planned for Apple Silicon with a macOS 14 deployment target, while the x86_64 convenience slice remains physically unverified; macOS 14 support still requires exact-candidate Sonoma lifecycle evidence. Comprehensive assistive-technology certification is neither claimed nor a release gate; keyboard behavior, accessibility names/values, and non-color cues remain required. Hardware-dependent rows may have mock/read-back evidence while remaining explicitly “not hardware-verified.”
